About Pingyms

Pingyms was created by co-founders Gamal Hassan and Abrar Ahmed in order to provide people the much needed service of finding their ideal gym. Users can select from a multitude of criteria not available online unless one is willing to sift through reviews for hours. Users as well as gym owners are able to upload information to the site for the benefit of everybody visiting.
